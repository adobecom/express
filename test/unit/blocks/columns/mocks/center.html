3 of 10: A mini campaign

Once your long-form content is done, use Adobe Express to create the promotion for it. Mix images, text and graphics to make a series of social graphics that tease users about what’s coming soon. You can even create posts with quotes from the piece itself, and the date the content will launch. Use Resize to make versions of your post in the correct aspect ratio for the various platforms you’re creating for and maximize your efficiency.
